The Best Italian Food in Morioh

Trattoria Trussardi is an authentic Italian restaurant founded by chef Tonio Trussardi. Hailing from Naples, Trussardi is a culinary master bringing his proud heritage and cuisine to Japan. Trattoria is not your typical restaurant. Here, we specialize in solving your medical problems with food. Diarrhea, cavities, dehydration or insomnia? Come to Trattoria where our food will heal your body with an exhilarating 6 course meal modeled after a formal Italian meal. DISCLAIMER: Trattoria Trussardi handles physical ailments not psychological. We cannot cure marital problems, self-esteem issues, or Post Traumatic Stress Disorders. If you are experiencing these problems, please seek a health care professional.
